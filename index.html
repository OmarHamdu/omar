Where Technology Meets Creativity

Hello, I'm Omar. A software engineer in the making and a Full Stack Creative developer.

I study Computer Science, and I have a special passion for transforming complex ideas into usable digital products. What sets me apart is my ability to see the complete picture of a project; I don't just write Backend and Frontend code, but I care about design aesthetics and user experience, and even the promotional video for the project.

My skills include game development with Unity & C#, web and app development with HTML/CSS/JS & React, and creative production with Premiere Pro & Photoshop.

I always strive to learn the latest technologies, from cybersecurity to the latest game engines, because technology doesn't stop, and neither do I.
